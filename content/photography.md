I shoot 35mm and medium-format films, 
mainly using my Pentax 645 and Pentax 67 cameras. 
I also shoot some digital photos with Ricoh GR3.
You can see more of my work on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/wming72). 

I develop and scan most of my films. 
I use a small co-developed python tool [NegativeFilmProcessor](https://github.com/HamRadioM/NegativeFilmProcessor) to preview negative conversions.
Proper conversion is done with [NEGATIVE LAB PRO](https://www.negativelabpro.com/).
