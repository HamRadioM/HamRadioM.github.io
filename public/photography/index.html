Photography

I shoot 35mm and medium-format films, mainly using my Pentax 645 and Pentax 67 cameras. I also shoot some digital photos with Ricoh GR3. You can see more of my work on Instagram .

I develop and scan most of my films. I use a small co-developed python tool NegativeFilmProcessor to preview negative conversions. Proper conversion is done with NEGATIVE LAB PRO .
